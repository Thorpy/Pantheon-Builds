⚔ Melee DPS — Saradomin root wins overall, but Bandos wins 10–25pt. The One-Handed Affinity node (id 319, cost 2) is the most valuable single node for 1H melee — it makes 1H weapons attack 1 tick faster, a ~+25% DPS increase. This is why the solver prioritises the Saradomin tree: it gives access to 1H Affinity, Sara Edge, Pious Fury, and the Giant Slayer chain for boss content. Flurry is good but not as strong as it sounds — it makes your next attack fire 1 tick sooner, not double-hit. Berserker only works with 2H weapons below 50 HP. If you use a 2H melee weapon, Bandos stays competitive much longer.

🔨 Hammer / Prayer DPS — Saradomin root. The Paladin keystone + Righteous Fury chain creates orbiting hammers with a max hit equal to 325% of your prayer bonus — these are separate AoE projectiles, not a buff to your main weapon. Important correction from v2: Devout Vessel gives bonus HP proportional to prayer bonus, not prayer damage. It has been moved to the Ironman build. Faith Surge (+25% prayer bonus stat) still matters because it amplifies the prayer bonus that scales Righteous Fury and Spear of Light. Pious Fury adds direct +1% per active prayer — very real DPS with 3+ prayers on. One-Handed Affinity is also included here since hammers are 1H.

🏹 Ranged DPS — Wing of Armadyl. The Eye of Armadyl chain gives +3% ranged damage per tile of distance (24% at max range), and Sniper (+7%) multiplies with it. Headshot (25% chance for 150% damage after 5 consecutive hits) is strong on sustained boss fights. Big Bad Bolts +18% max hit chance ≈ +9% average DPS. Ricochet bounce count matters for AoE content. Heavy Loose and Strong Pull are the direct damage backbone.

🔭 Magic DPS — Hand of Zamorak. Two major corrections: Crackling Staff halves powered-staff attack speed (e.g. 4-tick to 2-tick), which roughly doubles DPS — this is by far the highest value node in the magic tree. Chaos Caster fires 2 additional random elemental spells on every cast with no rune cost — potentially tripling your hits per cast. These two keystones dominate. Bloodletter (+25% all magic + lifesteal, costs 15 HP per cast) is strong if you can sustain. Forsaken Pact (+25% spells but no food healing) is discounted for the no-food tradeoff. Volatile Cast (+35%) costs a cast tick, netting only ~8% real DPS.

🔥 Berserker — Bandos root then Guthix. Critical constraint: Berserker only works with two-handed melee weapons below 50 HP. Frenzy upgrades it to +25% total. Berserker Echo (up to 24% chance for a free melee echo hit) is the compounding factor. Giant Slayer is strong here — +5% per tile of boss size means +15% vs a 3x3 boss, +25% vs a 5x5. Flurry at 50% proc means your next melee attack fires 1 tick sooner — good but not a double hit. Blindbag (15–45% chance for a bonus weapon hit) stacks on top of everything. The synergy between Flurry speed and Berserker+Echo means this build really rewards staying alive at low HP.

What changed in this version (v3)

  • Devout Vessel corrected: the description says “Bonus HP equal to X% of your prayer bonus” — it is survival HP, not a prayer damage multiplier. Moved out of Hammer DPS entirely and into Ironman/Sustain.
  • Flurry corrected: description says “your next melee attack fires 1 tick sooner” — not a double hit. At 50% proc on a 4-tick weapon this is ~+12.5% DPS, not +50%. Weight reduced from 1.2 to 0.25 per %.
  • Crackling Staff: description says “powered staves attack speed halved” — halving cast time roughly doubles DPS. This is now correctly treated as the highest-value magic node. Weight: 2.0 per tick saved (50 ticks × 2.0 = 100 score).
  • One-Handed Affinity: description says “1H weapons attack 1 tick faster” — saves 1 tick on a 4-tick weapon = +25% more attacks. Previously unweighted (wrong effect key). Now weighted at 25.0 flat. This makes it the highest-value node for 1H melee builds.
  • Chaos Caster: “fires 2 random elemental spells on every cast, no rune cost” — two extra hits per cast is enormous. Scored at 30.0 flat per echo. This now correctly dominates early magic builds.
  • Berserker restriction: only applies to two-handed melee weapons below 50 HP. Melee DPS profile adjusted; Berserker is now a dedicated 2H build.
  • Gale Roots added to Ranged and Slayer profiles. +3% per stack (5 stacks = +15%) maintained on a sustained target. Previously missing from weights entirely.
  • Giant Slayer added to Melee, Berserker, and Slayer profiles. +5% per tile of boss size. Assume average 3x3 boss = +15% for melee boss content.
  • All builds path-verified connected via iterative ILP cut constraints.
