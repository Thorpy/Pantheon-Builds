At low budgets the solver correctly prioritises 1H Affinity, Pious Fury, and prayer regen over Paladin.

How builds work — v5 notes

  • Keystones require ALL listed prerequisites, small/medium nodes need only one. This means Paladin (needing both Spear of Light branch and Defenders as Shields branch) costs 17pt minimum — it cannot appear in a 15pt build.
  • Sara and Bandos share 312 of 314 nodes. The root choice only affects which nodes cost fewer points to reach first. Sara wins for 1H melee because One-Handed Affinity is 2pt closer. Bandos wins for 2H/Flurry because those nodes are 2pt closer. The solver correctly picks the root that gives cheapest access.
  • Root switches (★ starred rows) are genuine findings — a different root gives cheaper access to the highest-scoring nodes at that exact budget.
  • Plateau rows mean the build is identical across that range — extra points only add DR, HP, or ammo regen that score zero for this archetype.
  • Devout Vessel = HP bonus from prayer bonus %, not prayer damage. Excluded from Hammer DPS, included in Ironman/Sustain.
  • Flurry = next attack fires 1 tick sooner, not a double hit. 50% proc on a 4-tick weapon = ~12.5% more attacks.
  • Crackling Staff halves powered-staff attack speed, roughly doubling cast DPS. Highest value magic node.
