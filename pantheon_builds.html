How these builds were found

  • Exact optimal via Integer Linear Programming (CBC solver), not greedy. Every budget tier solved independently across all 6 god roots.
  • Connectivity enforced — iterative "connectivity cut" constraints ensure every selected node is reachable from the god root through purchased neighbours only. Builds with disconnected islands are rejected and re-solved.
  • One god root per build — the other 5 roots are locked out in each solve.
  • Total tree cost is only 456pt — so at 150pt you're spending ~33% of the entire tree. Builds stop gaining new effects when the archetype's relevant nodes are exhausted (Magic DPS caps around 100-110pt; Hammer caps once all prayer/hammer nodes are taken).
  • ★ Root switch rows indicate the solver found a different god root outperforms the previous one at that budget for this archetype.
  • Scores are relative to each archetype's weighting — you cannot compare scores across archetypes.
