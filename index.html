Methodology

  • Exact optimal — Integer Linear Programming (CBC) solves every budget tier for every root. Not greedy.
  • Connectivity enforced — iterative cut constraints guarantee every node is reachable from the root through purchased neighbours only.
  • Weights reflect real DPS maths: direct max-hit % = 1.0 per %; Flurry = 1.2 per % (multiplicative compound); Berserker = 0.6–1.0 per % depending on expected uptime; crit = 0.5 per % (50% crit bonus assumed); conditional effects discounted by estimated uptime fraction; flat effects (Renewal heal, Cheat Death, etc.) scored by estimated fight impact.
  • Starred rows ★ indicate the solver switched to a different god root at that budget tier.
  • Total tree cost is 456pt — at 150pt you hold roughly one-third of all nodes. Several archetypes exhaust their relevant chains before 150pt (Magic ~50pt, Summoner ~55pt, Hammer ~65pt).
